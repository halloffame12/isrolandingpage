Mission Highlights

Chandrayaan-3

Active

Lunar exploration mission that successfully landed on the Moon's south pole region.

Launched: Jul 14, 2023

Chandrayaan-3

India's third lunar exploration mission, successfully landed near the lunar south pole on August 23, 2023.

Mangalyaan

Completed

India's first interplanetary mission to orbit and study Mars.

Launched: Nov 5, 2013

Mangalyaan

Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM) was India's first interplanetary mission, making ISRO the fourth space agency to reach Mars orbit.

RISAT-2B

Active

Radar Imaging Satellite for all-weather earth observation.

Launched: May 22, 2019

RISAT-2B

Radar Imaging Satellite with day-night, all-weather monitoring capabilities for agriculture, forestry and disaster management.

GSAT-30

Active

Communication satellite providing coverage over the Indian subcontinent.

Launched: Jan 17, 2020

GSAT-30

Communication satellite providing television, telecommunication and broadcasting services across India.

Shukrayaan-1

Planned

Upcoming orbiter mission to study Venus's surface and atmosphere.

Launch: Dec 2025 (Expected)

Shukrayaan-1

India's first mission to Venus, planned to study the planet's surface and atmosphere with advanced instruments.
